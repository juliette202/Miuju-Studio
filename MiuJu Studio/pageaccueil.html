Model of the year
Anok Yai

Beginning/career

Discovered in october 2017 during Howard university's homecoming week, she became famous thanks to the photographer Steve 'theSUNK' Hall who posted her picture on Instagram. She chose to sign a contract with NEXT Model Management. She then became the first Sudanese model to open a runway for Prada.

When you don't dress like everyone else, you don't think like everyone else.

-Anok Yai
